GET TO KNOW US

Est. 2022

Stage Fright was formed in 2022 when lead singer Chad and bassist Eduardo decided to start a band in their garage. Their first performance at a local park made them an instant hit, and from there, the journey began. After recruiting King Liz on guitar and Blond guy on drums, Stage Fright became the talk of the town. With worldwide recognition, their 2025 world tour marked a new chapter in their rise to fame.

MEET CHAD - Lead Singer

Chad's powerful vocals and songwriting abilities propelled the band into the spotlight.

King Liz - Guitarist

King Liz's exceptional guitar skills are the backbone of the band's sound.

MEET EDUARDO - Bassist

Eduardo's bass lines and leadership helped unite the band, earning him national recognition.
